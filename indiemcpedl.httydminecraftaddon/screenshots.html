Night Fury

Rideable: YES

Health: 100 - 150

Attacks: Melee, ranged (you can attack from a distance when ride but you will need a staff)

Taming: Approach the baby dragon or a non-aggressive adult (non-aggressive adults change their eyes when a salmon is on hand) and feed raw salmon until hearts come out

Spawn: Savannah, At Night (in all biomes)

Tip: bring enough fish to tame your dragon and watch out for aggressive dragons
